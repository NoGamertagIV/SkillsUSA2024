How to Donate

Your generosity can make a world of difference in the lives of dogs who embody the very essence of kindness, loyalty, love, and determination. At Amici Vecchi Dog Rescue, we understand the profound bond between humans and their canine companions. It's the gentle nuzzle after a long day, the unwavering loyalty in every wag of a tail, and the boundless love that fills our hearts. With your support, we can continue our mission of providing sanctuary and care to dogs in need. Your donation enables us to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs who have faced hardships and adversity. Whether they're seniors seeking a peaceful retirement or playful pups eager for a forever home, every dog we rescue embodies the qualities that make them cherished companions. Your kindness empowers us to make a difference, one dog at a time. Your generosity ensures that each dog receives the love, care, and attention they deserve as they await their second chance. Together, we can give these remarkable animals the opportunity to thrive and flourish in loving homes where they'll continue to spread joy, loyalty, and love for years to come. Donate today and become a part of our mission to celebrate the extraordinary bond between humans and dogs.
